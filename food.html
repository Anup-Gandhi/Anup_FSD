Famous Restaurants in Pune

Vaishali

Vaishali is an iconic restaurant located on Fergusson College Road. Known for its delicious South Indian cuisine, especially the crispy dosas and refreshing filter coffee, Vaishali is a favorite hangout spot for students and families alike.

Speciality: Dosas and filter coffee.

Good Luck Café

Good Luck Café, situated on Deccan Gymkhana, is one of Pune's oldest Irani cafes. It is renowned for its bun maska, Irani chai, and mouth-watering biryanis. The café has a nostalgic charm and is a must-visit for its old-world ambiance.

Speciality: Bun maska, Irani chai, and mouth-watering biryanis.

Blue Nile

Blue Nile, located near Pune Camp, is famous for its delectable biryanis and kebabs. This restaurant has been serving the people of Pune for decades and is known for its rich, flavorful Mughlai cuisine and spacious dining area.

Speciality: Biryanis and kebabs.

German Bakery

German Bakery, situated in Koregaon Park, is a popular spot for its wide variety of baked goods and continental dishes. Known for its cozy ambiance, this bakery offers everything from fresh bread and pastries to hearty breakfasts and savory snacks.

Speciality: Freshly made bakery items.

Kayani Bakery

Kayani Bakery, located on East Street, is famous for its legendary Shrewsbury biscuits and mawa cakes. Established in 1955, this bakery is an institution in Pune and is always bustling with customers eager to savor its freshly baked goods.

Speciality: Shrewsbury biscuits and mawa cakes.
